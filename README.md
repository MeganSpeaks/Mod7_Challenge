# Pewlett-Hackard Analysis

## Overview of the Analysis

The main goal of this project is to help Bobby's manager prepare for the coming mass retirement of existing employees, specifically determining the number of retiring employees per title and to identify employees who are eligible to participate in a mentorship program.

## Results

1.  The silver tsunami will hurt senior engineering and senior staff numbers the most.  29,414 senior engineers and 28,254 senior staff employees will be retiring.

2.  Only 279 senior engineers are eligible to be mentors and 439 senior staff.

3. The next tier affected the most are engineers and staff.  14,222 engineers and 12,243 staff are retiring.

4. Only 439 engineers and 285 staff are eligible to be mentors.

## Summary

- How many roles will need to be filled as the "silver tsunami" begins to make an impact? There will be 90,398 roles that will need to be filled: 29,414 senior engineers, 28,254 senior staff, 14,222 engineers, 12,243 staff, 4502 technique leaders, 1761 assistant engineers, and 2 managers.

- Are there enough qualified, retirement-ready employees in the departments to mentor the next generation of Pewlet Hackard employees? There are 1549 retirement-ready employees currently: 279 senior engineers, 439 senior staff, 406 engineers, 285 staff, 77 technique leaders, 63 assistant engineers, and 0 managers.  It appears there are not enough.
